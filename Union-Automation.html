Union Rule Automation

A framework for digitizing Collective Bargaining Agreements (CBA) to eliminate manual seniority calculations and grievance risks.

The Challenge of Manual Seniority

In most Ontario LTC facilities, the "Staffing Clerk" is the gatekeeper of union rules. When a shift opens, they must manually cross-reference seniority dates, accrued hours, and status (Full-Time vs. Part-Time) to determine who gets the first call.

"Manual call-outs are the #1 source of labor grievances in healthcare operations."

Logic Visualization

IF
Shift Vacancy Reported
AND
Staff Status == "Part-Time"
DO
Sort by [Seniority_Date] ASC

The BaseCare Automation Stack

Master Seniority Lists

BaseCare syncs directly with your payroll system (ADP, Ceridian, etc.) to maintain a live-updating seniority list based on hours worked or hire date. No more manual Excel updates every quarter.

// Seniority Sort Logic
SELECT staff_id FROM roster
WHERE status = 'PT' AND credentials = 'Valid'
ORDER BY accrued_hours DESC, seniority_date ASC;

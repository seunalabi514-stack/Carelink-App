Our Commitment to Privacy

Privacy is highly important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Carelink Healthcare Inc. collects, uses, stores, protects, and discloses information through our healthcare staffing and workforce management platform, including services provided through thecarelink.ca and thecarelink.app.

Carelink provides cloud-based workforce management tools and staffing services for long-term care providers, healthcare organizations, and related facilities across Canada and the United States.

As privacy laws and best practices evolve, we may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Information We Collect

Manual Submission

Information may be submitted directly through forms, email communication, uploaded documents, support requests, staffing applications, registrations, onboarding workflows, or direct communication with Carelink representatives.

  • Name and contact information
  • Email addresses and phone numbers
  • Facility or organization details
  • Billing and account information
  • Staffing credentials and compliance documentation
  • Healthcare-related operational information

Automatic Submission

When users interact with our websites or applications, certain technical information is automatically collected to support functionality, security, analytics, and platform performance.

  • Browser type and operating system
  • Device information
  • IP address and general geographic region
  • Session activity and visited pages
  • Cookies and analytics identifiers
  • Referral URLs and search terms

Cookies & Analytics

Carelink uses cookies and related technologies to improve usability, preserve session settings, authenticate users, and analyze platform usage trends.

We may use analytics providers such as Google Analytics to better understand website behavior and improve our products and services. These tools do not intentionally collect Protected Health Information (PHI).

Cookie Notice

Disabling certain cookies may impact access to authenticated platform functionality and workforce management features.

How We Use Information

Carelink uses personal and operational information for the following purposes:

  • Providing workforce management and staffing services
  • Supporting customer accounts and onboarding
  • Credential validation and compliance workflows
  • Scheduling, staffing, and operational automation
  • Customer support and troubleshooting
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Product improvement and analytics
  • Service announcements and updates
  • Research, benchmarking, and reporting using aggregated non-identifiable data

Protecting Health Information

Carelink customers may include healthcare providers and long-term care organizations that are subject to privacy and health information laws including HIPAA, HITECH, PHIPA, and related provincial or state legislation.

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Personal Information and Protected Health Information.

  • Encrypted communications using SSL/TLS technology
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Role-based access controls
  • Confidentiality agreements for employees and contractors
  • Firewall and infrastructure protections
  • Security monitoring and incident response procedures

Sharing Information

Carelink does not sell Personal Information.

Information may be shared with authorized third-party providers, integrations, or connected services where required to support platform functionality or where explicitly authorized by customers.

We may also disclose information where legally required, including court orders, subpoenas, or regulatory obligations.

Data Retention

Personal Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide services, fulfill contractual obligations, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

When retention is no longer necessary, information is securely deleted, destroyed, or anonymized where feasible.

Cross-Border Transfers

Carelink primarily operates from Ontario, Canada. Certain support, backup, or infrastructure operations may involve secure cross-border access or storage in compliance with applicable legal and contractual obligations.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on applicable law, individuals may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection regarding their Personal Information.

Requests regarding Personal Information or PHI may require verification and, in some cases, coordination with the applicable healthcare organization or customer account administrator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common support requests

How do I reset an employee login?

Navigate to the Personnel section within BaseCare Admin, select the employee profile, and choose “Reset Access”. A temporary authentication link can be sent by SMS or email.

Is Carelink PHIPA and HIPAA aligned?

Yes. Carelink uses encrypted infrastructure, access controls, audit logging, secure hosting environments, and operational safeguards designed for healthcare environments.

Can we import existing employee records?

Yes. Our onboarding team can assist with importing employee lists, credentials, seniority data, schedules, and historical staffing records.

Executive Summary

Healthcare labor compliance cannot scale through manual interpretation.

Unionized healthcare environments require consistent application of seniority, qualification, overtime, and assignment rules across complex staffing structures. Traditional scheduling methods rely heavily on human interpretation, increasing operational inconsistency and grievance exposure.

The Operational Risk

Schedulers manually evaluate eligibility, seniority ranking, qualifications, and assignment sequencing across multiple staffing pools and collective agreement structures.

The Infrastructure Shift

Union logic becomes encoded into deterministic workforce governance infrastructure capable of real-time assignment enforcement and audit traceability.

Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

Privacy is highly important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how BaseCare Healthcare Inc. (“BaseCare”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, protects, stores, and manages information entrusted to us through our healthcare staffing and workforce management platform and related services.

The BaseCare platform is designed for long-term care, healthcare staffing, workforce scheduling, credential management, and operational coordination within regulated healthcare environments.

Enterprise-grade trust

BaseCare is designed with healthcare-grade security, encrypted infrastructure, controlled access systems, and operational safeguards aligned with modern healthcare privacy standards.

What Is Considered Private?

Certain information may identify or distinguish an individual and is therefore considered private under applicable privacy laws.

  • Government-issued identifiers
  • Social insurance numbers
  • Billing and payment information
  • Email addresses and phone numbers
  • Healthcare and employment records
  • Credential and compliance documentation
  • Insurance and benefits information

Depending on jurisdiction, this information may be classified as Personal Information (PI), Personally Identifiable Information (PII), Sensitive Personal Information (SPI), or Protected Health Information (PHI).

Personal Information Collected by the Services

Manual Submission

Information may be submitted directly through account registrations, support requests, onboarding activities, uploaded files, or communications with BaseCare staff.

Automatic Submission

Certain technical information is automatically collected when interacting with the Site or Services.

Cookies & Sessions

We use cookies and session technologies to preserve secure authentication, maintain preferences, and improve platform performance.

Analytics

Usage analytics help us understand feature usage, reliability, performance, and operational improvements across the platform.

Purpose

BaseCare collects and processes information to provide healthcare workforce management and staffing services.

Customer Accounts

Registering and administering customer organizations and users.

Operational Services

Supporting scheduling, staffing, credential tracking, and compliance workflows.

System Improvements

Monitoring performance, reliability, audits, and service improvements.

Communications

Responding to support inquiries, updates, and service notifications.

Sharing Your Personal Information

BaseCare does not sell Personal Information.

Information may be shared with authorized service providers, integrations, legal authorities where required by law, or during business reorganizations subject to legal safeguards and contractual protections.

Security, Threats & Breach Notification

BaseCare maintains administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect against unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or alteration of information.

Encrypted Infrastructure

SSL/TLS encrypted communications and secure hosting environments.

Access Controls

Role-based permissions and authentication protections.

Secure Operations

Monitoring, firewall protection, and operational safeguards.

Retention & Deletion

Personal Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and support healthcare operational requirements.

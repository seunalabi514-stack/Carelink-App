02 "As of Right" Labor Mobility

Effective January 1, 2026

Out-of-province physicians and nurses are now registered under the Automatic Recognition Framework. Employers must ensure due diligence—validating out-of-province competency and site-specific hazard training within the first 30 days of hire.

  • No requirement for "Canadian Experience" in job postings.
  • AI in applicant screening must be explicitly disclosed.
